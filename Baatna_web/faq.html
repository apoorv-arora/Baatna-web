Frequently Asked

1. What does Baatna do?

Baatna is a platform that allows you to borrow things from people around you. You can post your need and borrow any consumer good such as - travel bag, gym equipment or juice maker etc. on the application. We will broadcast your needs to people in your neighborhood, your friends and other people in your community who can help you, you can then interact using our app and fulfill your need.

2. How does it work?

As you post your need on our app, it gets broadcasted as a push notification/email/sms on the mobile phones of all other relevant users in your community. Now, any member of your community who possesses the thing you need and is willing to share it with you can accept your request and you guys can then have a conversation via our app. Although we urge people to personally meet to pick up and drop things by themselves, we also provide on demand logistics facility.

3. How do you make sure that my need gets fulfilled?

We make sure that your needs get fulfilled in the following way-
  • As you post your need it gets broadcasted to people near you in your community.
  • Our algorithms make sure that your need also gets broadcasted to people in your Facebook friend list and your phone contact list in the right chronological order.
  • If still unfulfilled, our operations team will make sure that they notify people who have previously fulfilled similar needs.


4. Why would someone lend out their things for free?

People like offering help, from the goodness of their own heart (not because they are forced to). Baatna offers them an opportunity to do so, it helps people feel good about themselves, to feel happy that they helped someone. And this direct personal incentive directly contributes to these more abstract personal beliefs of creating a community and reducing waste.
On Baatna, you are asking known people inside your community for help. People are much more reluctant to ask a particular person for help (as required in the inventory based model), than asking for help in general (shouting out the request at Baatna).
The inventory model requires a user to reveal all items she/he is willing to lend to potential borrowers. This may lead to awkward situations wherein a lender might like to deny a request for a certain item from a particular borrower. People hate to say no, and therefore they may not put up a lot of things on display in their inventory. In Baatna, on the other hand, you may decide to lend something in response to particular request. You never have to say no, you just choose each and every time to say yes (or say nothing).

5. Why should I post my need on Baatna and not browse through Internet for it?

You don’t have to buy things in order to have access to them. No one uses an electric drill all the time, but buy one just for the occasional need at home. By borrowing rather than buying, you save money, and prevent yourself from becoming overburdened with stuff.
You get to meet people in your neighborhood. Remember the age old pretext of “borrowing a cup of sugar” in order to chat up that hot girl on the floor below? Well, Baatna allows you a whole bunch of new people to meet through borrowing something. Given that they own a specific item that you need, there’s a decent chance that you have some interest in common.
You are also doing good for the environment. By borrowing and lending things, fewer items are needed in a neighborhood. This prevents waste and the use of our planet’s valuable resources in producing things.

6. How can I be sure that the product I lend out is safe?

We will make sure there are no security issues by doing the following-
  • We focus on verifying people who use our app which ensures product safety.
  • We ask people to authenticate by logging in via Facebook.
  • In case you feel the thing you are lending out is too valuable, you can ask the borrower to setup a security amount with us which will be 100% refundable upon timely return.
  • Further, in case of any damage to the product you are lending, our consumer support team will make sure you are adequately compensated for it.


7. What cities do you currently operate in?

While we are currently active only in Delhi/NCR, you can very easily access it in your communities elsewhere as well. All you need to do is download our app on your phone and we will equip you with all the required things to get more people in your community on Baatna.

8. There are not many active users in my community. What do I do?

As mentioned earlier you can simply download our app and we will equip you with all the required things to be the pioneer member and get more people in your community on Baatna. By doing so we will also provide you with a pioneer badge in the application.

9. How do I post a need?

Go to the ‘make a request’ section on the top in our app and post your need. We have tried to keep it as simple as possible and it should not take you more than 10 seconds to post your need.

10. How many people get my query?

The following people will be notified about your need-
  • Active users on your Facebook friend list
  • Active users in your phone contact list
  • Other active users in your geographical community
  • If your need still goes unfulfilled, our app notifies it to people in neighboring communities as well.


11. What do I do if my need is not fulfilled by other users in my community?

If your need is not fulfilled in a certain specified period of time (this depends on the product that you need) you have an option to opt for Baatna fulfilment which enables you to rent out that thing at minimum rent from a nearby merchant enrolled with us.

12. I think what you guys are doing is great. How can I volunteer?

Drop us a mail at hello@baatna.com and we will equip you with the necessary things to make you a pioneer of spreading Baatna in your community.

13. Why a mobile application?

We chose a mobile app because it makes it easy to verify people attached to particular phone numbers. Further it makes it easy to keep track of a user’s geographical location via a mobile app which in turn helps us to broadcast your need in the right manner.

14. What is crowdfunding?

Crowdfunding is a method of raising capital through the collective effort of friends, family, customers, and individual investors. It is basically a way for people who are running projects that have a social value to raise funds from the aforementioned people.

15. How can I help you raise money?

We will launch a crowdfunding campaign very soon, you can go through our idea and contribute in whatever capacity you can. We will make sure to express our gratitude to you in the form of rewards even for the smallest of contributions.
Our aim is to disrupt the concept of ownership and bring a reform in the sharing space.
