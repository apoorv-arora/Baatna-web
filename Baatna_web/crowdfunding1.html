co-sharing app.

How it Works?

Unlike other inventory based co-sharing apps,with Baatna you can shout out for a need and have itfulfilled instantly by people in your community or neighbourhood

Sharing is good!

What if you and your neighbour had one lawn mower? Wouldn't it save resources for the second? in a world driven by consuming. Lets pause for a second & discover the joys of sharing.

  • Have short-term access to goods based on need

  • Sustainable consumption leads to a better environment

  • Saving cost by borrowing items

The Challenge

Our primary challenge is to kickstart a community and for that,
we need a small amout of investment to spread the idea in every area.


With whatever help we get, we will bring together a large number of smaller
communities that can interact with each
other using Baatna.
Our aim is to disrupt the concept of ownership and bring a reform in the sharing space.
