SITE DESCRIPTION

STARTink provides a socio-goal management platform. Just enlist an activity and set the timer Make a list of all that you wish to do in your life. See what your friends are u to, inspire them and get inspired.

All of us look back in time and wonder what we could have accomplished. Now is the time to plan, execute and INK. This is a portal for you and your friends to delve into an unexplored world of opportunities and acquire life changing experiences. Reenergize your thoughts and reanimate your life. Just go with your passion and don’t settle.

STARTink will not only help you achieve your goals but also help you remember your dreams. Let’s get you acquainted with startINK:

What is an INK?

An INK is any goal or activity that you wish to accomplish in a given period of time. You can set desired time for the goal you intend to accomplish.

What happens after I achieve a goal or task?

After you have achieved a task you just need to click “Accomplished” and you would have earned an authentication seal (inked image) on your activity note (insert an inked activity note img). You can additionally upload media(for now photos) in the section “I Made it Happen” to let your friends know about your accomplishments.

How do I add an INK from my friend’s page to my own to- do list?

We, at STARTink understand that friends share goals and dreams. If you want to add an INK from your friend’s page to your to do list, simply click on “Me Too”(insert img ) button on his activity note .

How can I help my friends accomplish their goals?

When you see a friend’s to-do list, you can click on the “Already done”button (insert img) if you have already accomplished that task. In this way, you can let your friend know that you have already done that task and help him/her to accomplish their goals.

What is a “Splash Sheet”?

A Splash sheet will demonstrate one’s progress on a committed goal. You can also comment and discuss about the task by using splash sheet.
